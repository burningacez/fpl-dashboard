Tie Breaker Hierarchy

Weekly Loser

  1. Fewest transfers that gameweek
  2. Coin flip

Manager of the Month

  1. Highest net score for the period
  2. Fewest transfers made during the period
  3. Highest single gameweek score
  4. Highest lowest scores (comparing lowest GW scores)
  5. Coin flip

Season (Final Standings)

  1. Fewest transfers over the season
  2. Most MotM wins
  3. Fewest weekly losses
  4. Highest single GW score over the season
  5. Highest of lowest scores over the season
  6. Coin flip
