Season 25/26 Rules

Entry & Buy-In

Each manager pays a £30 entry fee at the start of the season.

This contributes to the prize pool which is distributed based on league performance, Manager of the Month wins, and cup success.

Weekly Loser

The manager with the lowest score each gameweek is declared the Weekly Loser.

  • Weekly Losers pay an additional £5 fine per loss
  • This fine is added to the prize pool
  • Net points are used (after transfer deductions)

Manager of the Month (MotM)

The season is divided into 9 periods for MotM competition:

Period Gameweeks
Period 1GW 1-5
Period 2GW 6-9
Period 3GW 10-13
Period 4GW 14-17
Period 5GW 18-21
Period 6GW 22-25
Period 7GW 26-29
Period 8GW 30-33
Period 9GW 34-38

MotM Prize

Each period winner receives £30.

Tiebreakers

In the event of a tie, the following tiebreakers apply in order:

  1. Highest net score for the period
  2. Fewest transfers made during the period
  3. Highest single gameweek score
  4. Highest lowest scores (comparing lowest GW scores)
  5. Coin flip

Chips (25/26 Season)

Each manager receives 4 chips that can be used during the season:

  • Wildcard (WC) - Make unlimited free transfers
  • Free Hit (FH) - Make unlimited transfers for one gameweek only
  • Bench Boost (BB) - Points from bench players count
  • Triple Captain (TC) - Captain scores triple points
Important: For the 25/26 season, all 4 chips are available during the first half (GW 1-19) and then refresh for the second half (GW 20-38). Use them or lose them!

League Finish Prizes

At the end of the season, prizes are awarded based on final standings:

Position Prize
1st Place£320
2nd Place£200
3rd Place£120

Cup Winner

The FPL Cup winner receives £150.

Cup rules follow the official FPL Cup format with head-to-head knockout rounds.
